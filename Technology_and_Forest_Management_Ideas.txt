IDEAS ON HOW TECHNOLOGY CAN AID FOREST MANAGEMENT

1.  Satellite Monitoring Satellite imagery can help track deforestation,
    forest degradation, and land-use changes in real time.

2.  Drones for Surveillance Drones can monitor illegal logging, forest
    fires, and wildlife activity quickly and efficiently.

3.  Geographic Information Systems (GIS) GIS tools can map forest
    resources, analyze data, and support better planning and
    decision-making.

4.  Fire Detection Systems Early warning systems using sensors and AI
    can detect smoke or temperature changes to prevent large-scale
    forest fires.

5.  Mobile Data Collection Apps Forest rangers can use mobile apps to
    record tree species, track biodiversity, and report incidents
    instantly.

6.  Artificial Intelligence (AI) AI can analyze large datasets to
    predict fire risks, disease outbreaks, and climate impacts on
    forests.

7.  Internet of Things (IoT) Sensors IoT devices can monitor soil
    moisture, rainfall, and tree health to improve forest
    sustainability.

8.  Blockchain for Timber Tracking Blockchain technology can improve
    transparency in timber supply chains and reduce illegal logging.

9.  Reforestation Technology Seed-planting drones and automated
    nurseries can speed up reforestation efforts.

10. Community Engagement Platforms Digital platforms can educate
    communities, encourage reporting of illegal activities, and promote
    sustainable forest use.
